# IROL Field Call

An interactive learning experience for fire alarm field technicians on inspection reporting, customer communication, and code compliance. Built around a branching simulation that scores learner judgment on two axes (Compliance and Trust) and routes each learner to the remediation depth that matches their performance.

## What this is

IROL Field Call puts the learner inside a single service call. They walk through a real inspection sequence (the puff test, coverage evaluation, documentation, office submission), make decisions at branching moments, and live with the consequences of those decisions in the same simulation. The course is designed for technicians whose reporting habits affect both legal compliance (IFC 110.3.1, the 30+2 day timeline) and long-term customer relationships.

## How it works

**Module 1. Storyline simulation.** A branching scenario built in Articulate Storyline 360. Learner choices move two variables in real time: a Compliance score and a Trust score. The final results dashboard scores the learner on both axes and routes them into one of four tier paths.

**Module 2. Tier-routed Rise remediation.** Based on the simulation outcome, the learner opens one of four Rise courses. Each course is calibrated to the specific gap pattern the simulation surfaced.

## The four tier paths

- **Gold (Light Reinforcement).** Both Compliance and Trust at or above 85. Five to ten minutes. Reinforces the Decline of Service category, quote requirements, and handling mixed findings on a single call.
- **Silver (Communication and Trust).** Strong on compliance, lower on trust. Fifteen to twenty minutes. Covers reading the customer, the quote conversation, and closing a Decline of Service with grace.
- **Bronze (Compliance and Procedure).** Strong on trust, lower on compliance. Fifteen to twenty minutes. Covers the legal foundation, the puff test framework, coverage evaluation, and documentation rigor.
- **Red (Full Remediation).** Lower on both axes. Thirty to forty-five minutes. Integrates the Bronze procedural content with the Silver communication content plus GoCanvas service app practices.
